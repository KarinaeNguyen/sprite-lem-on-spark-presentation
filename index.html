SLIDE 2

MARKET LANDSCAPE & COMPETITION

Rivalry (HIGH)

7Up (45% share) dominates the street channel through strong localization and traditional taste fit (7-Up Lemon Soda). Sprite must win back share by owning a bolder emotional experience that 7Up has not built yet.

Substitutes (HIGH)

Health-conscious behavior and sugar-tax risk are pushing consumers toward alternatives such as fresh tea and kombucha.

Buyer Power (HIGH)

Gen Z has abundant beverage choices and high expectations for both emotional experience and brand authenticity.

New Entrants (MEDIUM)

Distribution scale remains a barrier, but niche local brands are emerging through food-delivery apps.

Supplier Power (LOW)

Sprite holds strong cost advantages from Coca-Cola's global operating scale.

Strategic Response

Compete head-on with 7Up by linking Sprite to street-food moments and using natural lemon refreshment to cut through greasy taste.

SLIDE 6

CUSTOMER VALUE PROPOSITION (CVP)

Heavy Food = Heavy Mood. Sprite releases that heaviness.

CUSTOMER PROFILE

  • Pains: Greasy fatigue and low energy ("Food Coma"). Limited safe, civilized spaces to wear expressive outfits (cosplay) without judgment.
  • Gains: A refreshed emotional state and a socially accepted trigger to express multiple identities.

VALUE MAP

  • Pain Relievers: Lemon refreshment that cuts through fatty taste and heaviness. "Coca-Cola World" creates clean, safe urban spaces that remove social hesitation.
  • Gain Creators: Spark positive energy and celebrate self-expression so Gen Z can confidently "Dress up Sparking".

SLIDE 7

FROM INSIGHT TO BIG IDEA: "LEM 'ON SPARK!"

Core Insight

Street food is delicious, but greasy meals leave Gen Z sluggish (Heavy Food = Heavy Mood). They need a spark to shake off the heaviness and reveal their vibrant inner identity (wibu/cosplay).

BIG IDEA

LEM 'ON SPARK! - AWAKEN FLAVOR, UNLOCK IDENTITY

The Ogilvy "Big Idea" Validation:

  • Brilliantly simple, simply brilliant: one sip of Sprite cuts greasy heaviness and triggers a transition into "Dress up Sparking" behavior.
  • Does it fit the strategy? Yes. It tightly connects the functional benefit (cutting greasy taste) with the emotional and cultural payoff (identity breakthrough).

SLIDE 11

B2C VIRAL ECOSYSTEM & B2B SUPPLY PRESSURE

B2C (Media Ignition)

  • Press amplification around the mission to elevate street-food culture.
  • Paid KOCs/KOLs activate street channels with "Dress up Sparking" content to generate FOMO waves.
  • "Sprite Always Supports" commitment: sustained creative support for food reviewers, KOL/KOC creators, and live food trucks for stable long-term output.

B2B (Supply-Side Pincer)

  • App-driven pull strategy: discounted GrabFood combos force vendors to stock Sprite to satisfy high Gen Z online order volume.
  • Vendor conversion play: once stocked, sales teams deploy Sprite/Lo Lem POSM assets (awnings, umbrellas, signage) to take visual dominance at point of sale from 7Up.

SPRITE CREATOR & FOOD TRUCK PLEDGE

Sprite maintains a generous, creator-first approach to the food community through ongoing support for reviewers, KOL/KOCs, and live food trucks via content funding, POSM, and on-ground activation support in key campaigns.

CREATIVE STRATEGY: TASTY EVENT

THINK-FEEL-DO: (THINK) Sprite cuts greasy heaviness -> (FEEL) Safe to express identity -> (DO) Order app combos, create TikTok content, and chase event access.

PLATFORM ECOSYSTEM:

PILLAR 1: YouTube (Pastime Context)

6-second bumper ads with ASMR carbonation sound design to interrupt greasy-food moments and trigger a refresh mindset (Think).

PILLAR 2: TikTok / Reels (Identity Context)

KOC-led AR filter transformation trend "Spark Your Style" that moves users from casual daywear to expressive cosplay identity (Feel & Do).

PILLAR 3: Out-of-Home (Trust Context)

Large-format billboard placements with ambassador Lo Lem to build trust and mass credibility at key urban traffic points.

EVENT UNIT ECONOMICS (CONSERVATIVE)

EXECUTIVE SNAPSHOT

Investment Cost per Event Participation

~155,555 VND/person

Formula: 700,000,000 / 4,500 = 155,555 VND/person

Potential Value Generated per Person

Decision View

Short-term ROI may be low (~0.77x), but this is a strategic retention investment in core users.

  • Lock existing customers deeper into the Sprite Community.
  • Increase business customer loyalty via food apps and street points of sale.
  • Lift LTV for future campaign cycles.

Decision line: short-term ROI may be lower, but each attendee represents a real customer with long-term annual value for Sprite to get attention and kick off changes.
